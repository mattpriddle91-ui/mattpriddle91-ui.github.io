Hospiscare Choir Welcomes You

Singing to support Hospiscare

Hospiscare Choir is an independent charity choir created in February 2015 by Brian Clifton, who worked in the Income Generation Team at Searle House Hospice, Exeter, the base of Hospiscare.

Members of the choir are staff, nurses, doctors, volunteers and supporters of Hospiscare, old and new. We sing an eclectic mix of popular songs and perform at Hospiscare events
